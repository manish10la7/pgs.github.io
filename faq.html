What is PGS App's core mission?

The Presidential Graduate App (PGS App) serves as a comprehensive one-stop solution for all college student needs, bridging the communication gap between college management and students while centralizing various student-centric functionalities.

Who are the target users?

Primary users are college students, with secondary users including college management and teachers. The app is designed to serve the entire academic community.

What technology powers PGS App?

PGS App is built using React Native and the Expo framework, ensuring a high-quality cross-platform experience. The backend is powered by Firebase, providing robust authentication, real-time data syncing, and secure data storage.

Which devices are supported?

The app is available on both iOS and Android platforms, supporting phones and tablets. The cross-platform development ensures a consistent experience across all devices.
